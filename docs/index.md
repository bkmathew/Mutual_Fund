# The Indian Mutual Fund Masterclass

### Zero to Expert — Investing, the Industry, the 2026 Law & the Problems

A study program that takes a reader from **zero** to **subject-matter expert** on mutual-fund investing in India: how funds work, who runs them, the **SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations, 2026**, and the real problems faced by investors and by fund houses.

!!! warning "Not investment advice"
    Every figure and fund name here is **illustrative** and clearly labelled. Nothing on this site is a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any specific scheme. Figures are current to **mid-2026** and sourced to AMFI/SEBI; they move over time.

## How the program is built

The curriculum climbs in five levels. Each module assumes only what earlier ones taught, defines every term on first use, and grounds every concept in a worked example with realistic Indian figures.

| Level | Focus | Modules |
|------|-------|---------|
| **L1 · Foundations** | The market snapshot, what a fund is, who runs it, what it costs | M0–M5 |
| **L2 · Practical** | The decisions and documents an investor actually faces | M6–M8 |
| **L3 · Analyst** | Risk-adjusted performance, attribution, debt analytics, entry/exit math | M9–M14 |
| **L4 · Industry & Law** | Construction, archetypes, industry economics, the 2026 Regulations | M15–M18 |
| **L5 · Problem Maps** | 25 investor problems + 25 fund-house problems | M19–M20 |
| **Capstone** | Model portfolio + Investment Policy Statement + outlook | M21 |

## Regulatory anchor

The program speaks the law of the **SEBI (Mutual Funds) Regulations, 2026** — approved 17 December 2025, effective 1 April 2026, replacing the 1996 Regulations. Each module's section 7 cites the applicable provision; **M18** is the dedicated deep dive.
